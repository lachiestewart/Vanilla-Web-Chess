Cashmere Chess Club

The Cashmere Chess Club is open to students of all ages and skill levels. Come along on a Tuesday or Thursday lunchtime in M3 to sign up and play. We are supervised by Mr Quaid and playing or touching the musical instruments while at Cashmere Chess Club is prohibited

Your club rating is calculated by your win/loss ratios and so ranks are given accordingly. Kings have a ratio of 10 or higher, Queens between 8 and 10, Rooks between 6 and 8, Bishops between 4 and 6, Knights between 2, and 4 and Pawns between 0 and 2
